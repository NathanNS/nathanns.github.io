About Devon

Background

Devon grew up in southern Ontario, Canada, where she enjoyed an active lifestyle from an early age by playing sports and spending time in the outdoors. She was first introduced to practising yoga after moving to British Columbia to study Psychology at UBC Kelowna. Devon began her yoga teaching career in 2018 by studying at and graduating from the Rishikesh Yogpeeth Yoga School in Rishikesh, India, where she achieved her 200 hour qualification in Hatha yoga. She subsequently completed her 300 hour qualification in Vinyasa yoga at Sampoorna Yoga in Goa, India which she graduated from in March 2020.

Teaching Style

In her classes, Devon loves to combine creative sequencing, a dose of inspiration and tons of gratitude and self love to help you deepen your practice. She seeks to help others thrive by nurturing their body, mind and soul with yoga. Her hope is that you step off the mat feeling refreshed, nourished and balanced.
